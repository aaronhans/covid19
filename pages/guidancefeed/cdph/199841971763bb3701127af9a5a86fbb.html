Governor Newsom Launches California Connected – California's Contact Tracing Program and Public Awareness Campaign

In the ongoing efforts to mitigate the spread of COVID-19, Governor Gavin Newsom today launched California Connected, the state’s comprehensive contact tracing program and public awareness campaign.
