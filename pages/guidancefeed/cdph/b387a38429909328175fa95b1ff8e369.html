State Officials Award Contract for New COVID-19 Data Reporting System to OptumInsight, Inc.

The California Department of Public Health (CDPH) and California Department of Technology (CDT) today announced that OptumInsight, Inc. has been selected to develop the state’s new COVID-19 data reporting system.
