Getting Vaccinated Against Flu More Important Than Ever

In an effort to prevent a "twindemic" of COVID19 and seasonal influenza, the California Department of Public Health (CDPH) is urging Californians to get vaccinated against the flu now to protect yourself and your loved ones.
