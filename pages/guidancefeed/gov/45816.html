Governor Newsom Announces Pilot Partnership with Biden Administration to Open Community Vaccination Sites in Los Angeles, Oakland 

Sites will serve as the pilot project for federal efforts to open vaccination sites nationwide These sites will provide targeted support to communities hit hardest by COVID-19 and its impacts OAKLAND – California Governor Gavin Newsom and the Biden-Harris Administration today announced a pilot project to establish community vaccination sites in Oakland and Los Angeles.
